Leave Earth Behind

The human species might as well call itself a success story. But its success is only grounded to its reach on the cosmic map. Humanity, in fact, has much farther to go. There’s no doubt that we will have to leave Earth at some point. The question we cannot decide on is when.

LEAP is the digital narrative that provides a vision of our future beyond Earth.
