THE FUTURE HAS LEFT US BEHIND

There is no doubt that humanity will eventually have to leave Earth. The question we cannot decide on is when. The end of the Space Shuttle program in 2011 has effectively extended our stay on Earth. How much longer must we remain on our planet before the choice isn't up to us anymore? The bigger question, why the need to wait at all?

LEAP is the digital narrative that provides a vision of our future beyond Earth.

Humanity has already demonstrated a sense of greatness regarding our first steps into space. It's time we uncover that sense of greatness again, to pose questions about our future and provide a platform on which those questions can be discussed.

The Earth has no conscience of its own, and space isn't calling out to us with a sense of immediacy either. If our future, that voice in our heads that tells us what to anticipate, is already telling us that we're leaving Earth, then we need to go where our future is headed, before it leaves us too far behind.

Look up at the stars and not down at your feet. Try to make sense of what you see, and wonder about what makes the universe exist. Be curious.

- Stephen Hawking

Future Hype

On the planet Tropsili VI, Alex Cuares awakens to the sound of hail pelting against his window. Hail is the primary form of precipitation on the planet. It’s a problem most colonists face on an almost everyday basis. That’s because Tropsili VI has twice the gravity of Earth. When hail falls, it accelerates at a rate greater than it does on Earth, doing far more damage on impact.
Alex Cuares is the mechanical engineer for his colony. It is a job that requires him to be outside, especially at times during intense periods of hail. Although his external suit is sufficient enough to protect him, Alex has to carry a supply of oxygen for his own life support, as well as his tools.
JanSport designed this biomimetic backpack with an accelerated, self-healing plastic casing, so every time hail hits his JanSport, the regenerative properties of the body itself will ensure full protection of what Alex stores inside it.
