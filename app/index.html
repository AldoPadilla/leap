Future Hype

On the planet Tropsili VI, Alex Cuares awakens to the sound of hail pelting against his window. Hail is the primary form of precipitation on the planet. It’s a problem most colonists face on an almost everyday basis. That’s because Tropsili VI has twice the gravity of Earth. When hail falls, it accelerates at a rate greater than it does on Earth, doing far more damage on impact.
Alex Cuares is the mechanical engineer for his colony. It is a job that requires him to be outside, especially at times during intense periods of hail. Although his external suit is sufficient enough to protect him, Alex has to carry a supply of oxygen for his own life support, as well as his tools.
JanSport designed this biomimetic backpack with an accelerated, self-healing plastic casing, so every time hail hits his JanSport, the regenerative properties of the body itself will ensure full protection of what Alex stores inside it.
